Sales Prediction Project

About This Project

This project is designed to help businesses predict future sales based on their historical sales data. By inputting sales data in a CSV file along with specifying the date and sales fields, our algorithm generates a sales prediction to assist in planning and strategy.

How It Works

Users upload a CSV file containing their historical sales data. They then specify which fields represent the date and sales figures. Our system processes this data using advanced predictive algorithms to forecast future sales.

Getting Started

To get started with our sales prediction tool, simply upload your sales data in a CSV format and follow the on-screen instructions to input the necessary field information.
